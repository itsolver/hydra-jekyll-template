--- title: Pricing heading: Get premium support description: faq: - question: What is a user? answer: A user is defined as one personalized email address (you@yourcompany). Group email aliases such as sales@yourcompany and support@yourcompany are included in pricing and don't count as additional users. - question: Do you offer email accounts? answer: Yes. You can purchase email accounts via G Suite from IT Solver. This also includes Google Calendar, Docs, Drive, and more. - question: How do I buy and pay? answer: Get started with a free 14-day trial or contact sales. After the trial ends, you can pay by credit card. - question: What is the payment plan? answer: We offer a flexible payment plan. You can add and delete users at anytime. You are billed monthly. For more information on pricing, contact sales or see the IT Solver Help Centre. - question: When are hourly rates billed? answer: Projects or work related to an add, remove or change. - question: What are the hourly rates for project work? answer: $100 an hour for customers on a plan, otherwise $150 an hour. 1 hour minimum, then billed in 15 minute increments. - question: Have more questions? answer: Angus usually responds in under an hour during Mon-Sat 9AM - 5PM. Visit the Help & Support Center to contact Angus. ---
